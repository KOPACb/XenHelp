Autostart

Autostart.py provides autostart for you VM`s running under Citrix Xen Server

$ crontab -e

$ 0-59 * * * * /script/autostart.py

make shure that your scripts isn`t use /tmp/autostart.pid

simply add tag autostart to your VM`s that need to be autostarted
