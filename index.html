Autostart

Autostart.py provides autostart for you VM`s running under Citrix Xen Server

1) copy script to your server and add in to crontab

$ chmod +x /path/to/autostart.py
$ crontab -e

 0-59 * * * * /path/to/autostart.py

2) make shure that your scripts isn`t use /tmp/autostart.pid or change 

pidfile = "/tmp/autostart.pid"

as your wish

3) simply add tag autostart to your VM`s that need to be autostarted

4) ...

5) PROFIT!
